Australian Animals Catalogue

Welcome to the Australian Animals Catalogue! This is a database constructed by the Australian Government in order to raise awareness of local Australian wildlife to those who are new to the country.

We present information about the local fauna under 6 main categories as follows.

  • Birds
  • Mammals
  • Fish
  • Reptiles
  • Amphibians
  • Arthropods

If you have an encounter with any of the wildlife shown on this site, we would love to hear from you! Use the Submit Sightings page to tell us about your encounter.
